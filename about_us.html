ABOUT BRAKE BUDDY

What?

Brake Buddy is the brake light of the future. It is the first brake light system with progressive lighting. This allows drivers to gauge how hard the car in front of them is braking in order to improve reaction time and brake accordingly.



How?

Brake Buddy utilizes progressive LED technology to expand high mount stop light the harder the driver pushes on the brake. The above graphic shows Brake Buddy with light (left) versus hard (right) braking force.



Why?

Brake Buddy’s goal is to create a revolutionary brake light system to decrease rear-ended accidents and make the roads safer.
