THE BRAKE LIGHT OF THE FUTURE

Brake Buddy exists to prevent rear-ended accidents and make the roads safer for all.


Brake Buddy is a manufacturer and distributor of brake light kits for automobiles. Our products are differentiated because our kits use specialized technology to allow trailing cars in traffic to gauge the force on the brake from the car in front of them.


We are a company that supplies our products to car manufacturers all over the US and also sells individual units in stores like Home Depot and ACE Hardware. The culture of Brake Buddy is prioritizing the safety of the customers over profit. Our company culture is also technological and innovative with our slogan being “The brake light of the future.”
